Item Details

Berserker's Fury

Unique Passive - Fury: Increases Physical Attack by 40%. Increases Critical Strike Chance by 5%. Critical Strike Damage increased by 25% for the next 3 seconds.

Malefic Roar

Unique Passive - Devour: Increases Physical Penetration by 35% against heroes. Increases Physical Attack by 20% against enemies with 100% or higher Physical Defense.

Windtalker

Unique Passive - Wind Blast: Increases Attack Speed by 40%. Adds a Wind Blast attack that deals Physical Damage and slows enemies by 40%.

Bloodlust Axe

Unique Passive - Bloodlust: Grants 15% Spell Vamp for 5 seconds after dealing damage to an enemy hero. Increases Physical Attack by 10% for the next 5 seconds.

Golden Staff

Unique Passive - Energy Surge: Increases Magic Power by 20%. When using an ability, increases the next basic attack damage by 100% of Magic Power.

Immortality

Unique Passive - Resurrect: After being eliminated, the hero will revive with 15% of their Max HP and Shield.

Radiant Armor

Unique Passive - Radiant: Increases Armor by 100. Reflects 50% of Physical Damage received back to attackers.

Athena's Shield

Unique Passive - Shield: Increases Magic Defense by 90. Creates a shield that absorbs 200 points of damage from enemy abilities.

Queen's Wings

Unique Passive - Vitality: Increases Physical & Magical Defense by 25. Increases HP Regen by 20%. When HP is below 40%, increases HP Regen by 100% and Magic Defense by 100.

Thunder Belt

Unique Passive - Conduction: Increases Physical Defense by 40. Deals 200 points of Magic Damage to enemies within 400 range, slowing them by 40% for 2 seconds.

Genius Wand

Unique Passive - Genius: Increases Magic Power by 75. Decreases the target's Magic Defense by 30% after each ability.

Enchantress Blessing

Unique Passive - Blessing: Increases HP Regen by 150. Increases Magic Defense by 150. Increases Spell Vamp by 5% for 10 seconds after using an ability.

Fleeting Time

Unique Passive - Momentum: Reduces Ultimate CD by 25%. Increases Movement Speed by 5% for 2 seconds after using Ultimate.

Talisman of Beastly

Unique Passive - Blessing: Increases HP by 250. Increases Max Mana by 100. Increases Magic Power by 40.

Oracle

Unique Passive - Faith: Increases HP Regen by 100. Increases Magic Defense by 30. Increases Shield by 15% and HP Regen by 15% for 3 seconds.

Hunter's Knife

Unique Passive - Hunt: Provides 50% additional Gold for jungle monsters and 20% additional Gold for hero kills.

Giant's Kill

Unique Passive - Annihilation: Increases Physical Attack by 20%. Increases Physical Penetration by 15% against enemies with HP above 50%.

Beast Killer

Unique Passive - Hunt: Increases Physical Attack by 25% against monsters. Reduces the cooldown of skills after killing a monster.

Demon Hunter Sword

Unique Passive - Demonic: Reduces HP of enemies by 3% on each attack. Increases Physical Attack by 30% for the next 3 seconds.

Spell Vamp

Unique Passive - Bloodthirst: Increases Spell Vamp by 15%.
